About Course Instructor


Dr. Jonathan V. Taylar

Dr. Jonathan V. Taylar received his Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering (BS CpE) degree from the AMA Computer College, East Rizal, Antipolo, in 2006, and the Master of Science in Computer Science (MSCS) degree from AMA University, Quezon City, Philippines, in 2008. He recently graduated with a Doctor of Engineering Major in Computer Engineering (DENGG) degree from the Technological Institute of the Philippines, Quezon City campus.

His career in the academe started in 2006 after graduating from college. After two years of joining the AMA Education System, he worked for six years as an Information Technology Instructor for the General Secondary Vocational Education Certificate (GSVEC) in the Ministry of Education, Kingdom of Bahrain. He worked as an IT Lecturer for a year at TQ College of Excellence, Pearson Company in Saudi Arabia.

Since 2015, he has been an Assistant Professor with the Computer Engineering Department, T.I.P. Quezon city campus. He handles computer programming, software design, database management systems, CISCO networking, systems administration, and data science courses in the undergraduate and is also a part-time instructor handling Advanced Software Systems, Applied Machine Learning, and Special Topics in Data Science in the T.I.P. Graduate Studies programs for Quezon City and Manila campuses.
